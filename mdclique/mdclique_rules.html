ABOUT

WHAT IS THIS? :: MDClique is a Pixelart clique started by MiLKY-P, inspired by and now partnered with CDwORLD!

WHY? :: Honestly, i think MiniDiscs dont get enough love and to show my appreciation, i have decided to make my own Pixelart clique for the lil' discs.

CAN I JOIN? :: Sure! Just follow the rules and the manual, and then send me your submissions and i'll put you on here!

RULES

1. :: Please follow obvious stuff like being polite, not hateful, and patient.

2. :: Create the pixel art yourself please, stealing is not okay. You can use external resources.

3. :: The MiniDisc can be anything you want! Your favourite albums, mixtapes, or even blanks! Just uh, refer to the guide below.

3a. :: The MiniDiscs cannot be

1. :: Kanye West - Yandhi

2. :: Any variant of the MUJI 80 MiniDisc (MUJI 74 ok!)

4. :: Please follow the guideline of the MiniDiscs as seen in the templates. The final size of your MiniDisc should be 89x86 px.

5. :: Display the MiniDiscs as you like, whether it's just the pixel art or the relevant CD info too. Link back to the site (where the creators are credited) or directly to the creators.

6. :: Enjoy yourself!

:: MKT

HOW-TO!

1. :: Chose whether you will make a fanwork of an existing album or create a fictional album or a mixtape.

2. :: Download the templates from the template! You can choose the recordable clear one or the commercial one for albums!.

3. :: Add your own spin to the template! Change the color, add a label, add a sticker, change the disc!

4. :: If you're making a playlist or fictional album, make sure the total length fits on your chosen kind of MiniDisc. You can find more resources at the MiniDisc Wiki

5. :: Add it to your page somewhere and then contact me with the link and your name!
