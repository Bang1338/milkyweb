music i like

KW - Yandhi

Honestly, it's a pretty good album. The instrumentation is spectacular. The verses are also catchy and earworm-y as all hell. The sounds that were played with in this album are so bright and shiny. Could have been a modern day Graduation. Alas, as all good albums by Yeezy go, it went in the vault. Thank God For Leaks.


Favorite Track/s: Law Of Attraction

tv room - Dear Pet Rock

The latest single off tv room's latest album, "The Big Big Deal", and... wow! This song brings so much childlike whimsy whilst also showing the pains of growing up past your childhood and eventually yearning to memories long past. It gives such a melancholy feeling but still also manages to juxtapose it with the fun instrumentation and the lines on perserverance, like the writer has come to peace with their childhood and embraced their inner child.


すとぷりちゃんねる - Strawberry Prince

For the self-titled album of my favorite idol group, it perfectly encapsulates their golden era of music, when it was so distinct from most idol-adjacent music! Rinu's voice in particular, is well represented in the album. Nanamori and Jel's rapping during track breakdowns also give such an energetic burst to the poppy and bouncy tracks! Production is stellar on this album, and the soundscapes are very well executed in this album, taking influence from EDM, trap, orchestral, cinematic, synthpop, classical, pop-punk, DnB, RnB, and even Future Garage!


Favorite Track/s: じゃむじゃむシグナル, 四季, Streamer, Very, Prince

Mabisyo - Hentai Jazz Deluxe

Despite the title and album art, this album reminds me of traversing empty worlds in VRChat, the echoey samples mixed with the snappy snares just make this album so unique. It feels like i'm 7 and lost in the electronics section of the department store, and everything is just covered in frutiger aero.


Favorite Track/s: Goodbye Hugs Hurt More Than Kisses, Shared Dreams of a Cold Night

Michael Jackson - Thriller

Absolutely phenomenal album, and a pop icon. All of the tracks are bangers. Absolute classics. From MJ’s smooth voice to the punchy drums and synths, and the accompaniment of the guitars, a timeless classic.


Favorite Track/s: Pretty Young Thing

PAS TASTA - GOOD POP + GOOD POP THE REMIXES

This album takes a super unique take on the classic JPop band formula by injecting the influence of Hyperpop and EDM. It takes strong influence on the indie music scene aka the web music scene. It’s unlike any J-Pop album i’ve heard before. The remix album additionally adds so much sheer hype and energy to this already bubbly and cute album.


Favorite Track/s: sunameri smoke, peanut phenomenon (six impala remix)

Moe Shop - Moe Moe

This album achieves such a stuttery effect with its production that just scratches that itch in my brain just right. The smooth Japanese rapping floats like butter over the future-funk-esque production.


Favorite Track/s: Notice

Daft Punk - Alive 2007

I consider this one of the best live albums ever. It blends so many Daft Punk classics together, in a way that not only respects the production of the originals but enhances them. It’s a masterpiece all on it’s own. The accompanying live concerts were also visually stunning, and have aged quite well.


Favorite Track/s: Touch It/Technologic, Prime Time Of Your Life/Brainwasher/Rollin’&Scratchin/Alive

SOPHIE - Oil Of Every Pearl's Un-Insides

This album was an awakening for me. I put very high respect to this album, as it made me see a new vision both as an artist and as a person.


Favorite Track/s: Ponyboy

KW - Donda (Deluxe)

This album gave off such an airy vibe, and the production is great. The use of organs and choirs from the JIK era also elevates the soundscape of this album. Also, globglobgabgalab.


Favorite Track/s: 24, Remote Control Pt. 2

garlagan - 00

This album is so fun to listen to, it combines the guitars and pop rock influences of tracks like Do and Accelerated A, whilst also adding the Electro influences of tracks like Slowing Down and Who Knows. Bonus points for being on vivid/stasis!


Favorite Track/s: Holography

Madeon - Good Faith

This album just gives such a soulful rhythm. The production and vocals are amazing and the soundscapes give off this sparkly, warm, aura. Like hot chocolate with glitter.


Favorite Track/s: Gonna Be Good (Live), Be Fine

Venetian Snares - Detrimentalist

If i could describe ADHD as an album, this is it. The use of so many sounds, contrasted with the erratic drum breaks and off time signatures make this a unique listen and an exercise in thinking.


Favorite Track/s: Circle Pit, Sajtban
